Biology Courses

Pagdurusa University's Biology courses offer an interactive, experiential education. Students gain a direct understanding of organisms through cutting-edge dissection labs and virtual tools. Skilled educators offer direction and assistance, and students have the opportunity to conduct research.

Research Opportunities: Participate in independent or collaborative research projects with faculty support.

Career Guidance: Receive insights into various biology-related fields through guest lectures, workshops, and internships.
