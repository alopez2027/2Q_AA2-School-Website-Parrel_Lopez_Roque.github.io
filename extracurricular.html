Extra-Curricular Activities

Sports Club

Join our sports club to stay active, healthy, and socialize with other students. We have a variety of sports including badminton, basketball, and volleyball.

Art Club

Discover your artistic talent in our art club. We welcome students interested in digital art, traditional art, and crochet.

Science Club

Explore the fascinating world of science through experiments, projects, and discussions. Join us to nurture your curiosity.

Music Club

Discover your musical talent by joining our club. We welcome students interested in playing instruments (piano, guitar, and drums) or singing.
